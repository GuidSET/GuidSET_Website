Our product roadmap

ThoraSET

Pressure‑sensing trocar and sheath designed to differentiate chest wall, pleural cavity, and vital organs—aiming for first‑pass success in tube thoracostomy.

  • Integrated pressure sensing
  • Compact, rugged design
  • Prototype validation underway

OptiSET

Optical imaging integrated with pressure sensing to visualize tissue transitions and enhance safety margins during insertion.

  • Tissue‑layer visualization
  • Real‑time signal fusion
  • Human‑factors‑informed UI

Guided AI (Future)

Data‑assisted guidance to support clinicians—combining pressure, video, and context for intelligent prompts and post‑event reports.

  • On‑device inference
  • Procedure analytics
  • Workflow integration
